## ğŸ¨ Logo Design Tips

### **What Makes a Great Tech Logo:**

1. **Simple & Clean**
   - Not too many details
   - Easy to recognize
   - Memorable

2. **Scalable**
   - Looks good at 16px (favicon)
   - Looks good at 1000px (billboard)
   - Clear at any size

3. **Colors**
   - Orange = Energy, innovation, creativity
   - Dark Gray = Professional, stable, trustworthy
   - White space = Clean, modern

4. **Font**
   - Sans-serif (clean, modern)
   - Bold or semi-bold
   - Good spacing
   - No script/fancy fonts

5. **Icon/Symbol**
   - Related to tech (circuit, code, connection)
   - Related to name (Gebeta = board game)
   - Abstract geometric shape
   - Letter "G" stylized
